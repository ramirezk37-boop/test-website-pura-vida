What We Do

Comprehensive Furniture Care

From a single scratch to a full restoration — we handle it all with expert craftsmanship.

Wood Repair & Refinishing

Scratches, gouges, water stains, and worn finishes — we restore wood surfaces to their original beauty using professional-grade techniques.

Upholstery Repair

Torn seams, worn fabric, broken springs — we re-stitch, re-stuff, and re-cover your sofas and chairs to feel and look brand new.

Structural & Frame Repair

Wobbly legs, broken joints, cracked frames — we reinforce and rebuild furniture structure so your pieces are sturdy and safe again.

Touch-Up & Color Matching

We precisely match stains, paints, and finishes to blend repairs invisibly — even on antiques or custom-finished pieces.

Antique Restoration

We treat heirloom and antique pieces with the reverence they deserve — preserving character while restoring function and structural integrity.

In-Home & Shop Service

We come to you for convenient on-site repairs, or you can bring larger pieces into our workshop for a full restoration treatment.
